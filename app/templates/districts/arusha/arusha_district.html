Arusha District

Location

Arusha District is located in the Arusha Region of northern Tanzania. It is known for its proximity to Mount Meru and the Serengeti National Park.

Cost of Living

The cost of living in Arusha District is moderate. Housing and food costs are relatively affordable, but prices can vary depending on the proximity to tourist areas.

Available Economic Activities

  • Agriculture: Coffee and flower farming are prominent.
  • Tourism: Safari tours and mountain climbing expeditions.
  • Trade: Local markets and craft shops.
